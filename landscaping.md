### Key Decisions

#### Fencing: Cedar vs Pressure-Treated Wood
**Decision:** Cedar

| Factor | Cedar | Pressure-Treated |
|--------|-------|------------------|
| Aesthetics | Superior natural look | Greenish tint, less attractive |
| Durability | 15-20+ years | 10-15 years |
| Maintenance | Weathers to gray naturally, optional sealing | Requires regular treatment |
| Cost | Higher upfront | Lower upfront |
| Chemicals | Natural rot resistance | Treated with preservatives |

**Rationale:** Better long-term value, superior appearance, worth the upfront cost difference.

#### Irrigation
